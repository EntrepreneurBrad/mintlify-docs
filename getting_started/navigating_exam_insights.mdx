## Home Page

When you sign in, you will land on the [Home Page](https://exam-insights.com/home/). This is where all your subjects are displayed in one place, allowing quick access to any subject you are studying.

\{image}

## Subject Home Page

Clicking on a subject will take you to its [Subject Home Page](https://exam-insights.com/physics/home/). For this example, we are using physics.

\{image}

On the Subject Home Page, you’ll find an overview of your progress in that subject, including a graph that tracks the number of questions you’ve answered over time and your average rating for those questions.

Additionally, the Subject Home Page serves as a hub for all subject-specific features, including:

* Explore

* Questions

* Insights

* Performance

* Glossary

* Formula and Data Book (for applicable subjects)

### Explore

\{image}

[**Explore**](https://exam-insights.com/physics/explore/) allows you to navigate the QCAA syllabus, helping you find questions, exam trends, and monitor your performance to focus your study effectively.&#x20;

For a detailed guide on making the most of this feature, check out our [Explore walkthrough](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/explore_walkthrough).

### Questions

\{image}

[Questions](https://exam-insights.com/physics/questions/) provides a comprehensive collection of past QCAA exam questions, with filtering options to help you quickly find specific questions.&#x20;

For a detailed guide on making the most of this feature, check out our [Questions walkthrough](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/questions_walkthrough).

### Insights

\{image}

[<u>Insights</u>](https://exam-insights.com/physics/insights/) provides data-driven analysis of past QCAA exam questions, helping you identify trends and optimise your study approach.&#x20;

For a detailed guide on making the most of this feature, check out our [Insights walkthrough](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/insights_walkthrough).

### Performance

\{image}

[**Performance**](https://exam-insights.com/physics/performance/) provides real-time data on your strengths and weaknesses, helping you focus on the areas that matter most.&#x20;

For a detailed guide on making the most of this feature, check out our [Performance walkthrough](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/performance_walkthrough).

### Glossary

\{image}

[**Glossary**](https://exam-insights.com/physics/glossary/) allows you to easily find and understand key terms and concepts from the QCAA syllabus.

### Formula and Data Book

\{image}

[Formula](https://exam-insights.com/physics/formula-book/)[ and Data Book](https://exam-insights.com/physics/formula-book/) provides quick access to the QCAA Formula and Data Book.&#x20;

To see how Formula AI can enhance your experience with formula books, check out our [AI Tutor walkthrough](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/ai_tutor_walkthrough).

## Account Settings

The [Account Settings](https://exam-insights.com/settings/) button is always accessible on the bottom left of the page in the sidebar, no matter where you are on the site.

\{image}

In Account Settings, you can:

* Edit your account information (visit our [Updating Account Information page](https://help.exam-insights.com/troubleshooting/changing_account_details) for more details)

* Change your plan (visit our [Changing Your Plan page](https://help.exam-insights.com/troubleshooting/plan_change) for more details)

* Reedem access codes (visit out [Reedeming Access Codes page](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/access_codes) for more details)

* Update your subjects

* Reset Your Password (visit out [Account Access & Sign-In Issues page](https://help.exam-insights.com/troubleshooting/accessing_accounts#resetting-your-password) for more details)

* Log out

## Help Center

The [Help Center](https://exam-insights.com/help-center/) is located just above the Account Settings button in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar.

\{image}

It provides quick access to key resources, including:

* **Docs & Guides** – offering step-by-step instructions and explanations.

* **FAQs** – Answers to common questions about using the platform.

* **Pricing Details** – Information on subscription plans and costs.

* **Contact Us** – A direct way to reach our support team for further assistance.
