Syllabus is your interactive roadmap to mastering a subject, giving you full access to the official QCAA syllabus in a structured and intuitive way. It goes beyond a simple outline, providing dedicated pages for every unit, topic, sub-topic, and learning objective.

With everything connected, you can focus on what matters most—finding the right questions ([Questions](/getting_started/questions)), tracking your progress ([Performance](/getting_started/performance)), and gaining deeper insights ([Insights](/getting_started/insights))—all in a single, streamlined hub built for effective learning.

***

## Where to Find Syllabus

Finding the Syllabus page is simple, with two easy ways.

<Tip>
  Want a better understanding of how Exam Insights is structured? Check out our [Quick Start Guide](/getting_started/quick_start_guide) to see how our pages are organised and where to find key features.
</Tip>

### Option 1: From the Homepage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your My Subjects (Home)">
    This is where all of your enrolled subjects are listed.  

    <Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Subject">
    Click on the subject you want to view Syllabus for.

    <Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Syllabus">
    Once inside the subject, you'll see the *Syllabus*.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option 2: Using the Sidebar

**AI Syllabus Assistant**

<Note>  
  Use this 
</Note>

AI Syllabus Assistant is your personal assistant for understanding the syllabus. If you have any questions about a Subject Matter—whether it’s about what a concept means, how it applies in exams, or how to approach studying it—AI Syllabus Assistant provides instant answers. Simply ask a question, and AI will break it down in a way that’s clear and relevant to your studies.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
</Frame>

**Insights**

<Note>  
  Use this 
</Note>

Insights uses data from past QCAA exam papers to highlight exam trends related to the chosen Subject Matter. This can include how often a concept has appeared in past exams, common question formats, and key themes to focus on. By reviewing these insights, you can get a better idea of what to expect and how to prepare for future assessments.

**Questions**

<Note>  
  Use this 
</Note>

Questions only displays the exam questions that relate directly to the selected Subject Matter, making it easy to focus your practice on the exact topic you’re studying. It retains all the powerful features of the Questions tool, including:

* **Sorting** – Arrange questions by difficulty, year of publication, or in a random order

* **Search** – Use the search bar to quickly find questions that include specific keywords
