You can explore most of Exam Insights without an account, but creating one is the best way to personalise your experience for free and unlock extra benefits.

Signing up is quick and easy—it only takes a minute, and your account is completely free forever, with no credit card required. 

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Sign-Up Page">
    Start by heading to our [Sign Up](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/signup/) page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a Sign-Up Method">
    You can create an account using one of these secure options:  

    * Sign Up with Email and Password

    * Sign Up with Google

    <Check>
      Both options are secure—pick whichever works best for you. 
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Profile">
     To personalise your experience, you’ll go through a quick setup where we’ll ask for:  

    * Your Name

    * Your School

    * Your Subjects

    * Your Role (i.e. student, educator)

    * Your Upgrade Code (if supplied by your school)
  </Step>

  <Step title="You're All Set!">
    Your account is all set up—jump in and start exploring everything Exam Insights has to offer! 
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Not sure where to start? Check out our [**Navigating Exam Insights**](/getting_started/navigating_exam_insights) guide for tips on making the most of your experience!
</Tip>
