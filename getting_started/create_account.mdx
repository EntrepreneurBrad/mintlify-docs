<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Sign-Up Page">
    To get started, visit our [Sign-Up page](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/signup/).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a Sign-Up Method">
    You can create an account using one of the following methods:

    * Sign Up with Email and Password

    * Sign Up with Google

    <Check>
      Both sign-up methods are highly secure
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the Onboarding Process">
    Once your account is created, you’ll be guided through a quick setup process. You’ll need to provide:

    * **Your Name**

    * **Your School**

    * **Your Subjects**

    * **Your Role**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Using Your Account">
    After completing the onboarding process, your account will be fully set up. You can now explore all the features available on Exam Insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Support

If you run into any issues while creating your account, feel free to [contact our support team](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/).
