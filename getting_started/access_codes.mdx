If you’ve received an access code, there are two ways to redeem it, depending on whether you’re a new or existing user:

## Redeeming Access Codes for a New User

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    To create an account visit our [Sign-Up page](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/signup/). If you need further assistance see our [Creating Your Account guide](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/create_account).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply Access Code during Onboarding">
    During onboarding, you’ll find a section to enter your access code. Enter the code and click Apply to redeem it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Once you have completed the onboarding, you’ll be redirected to the home page, and your account will be upgraded instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Redeeming Access Codes for an Existing User

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Your Account">
    Log in to your account/the account you want to redeem the access code on.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Account Settings">
    In the bottom left corner of the sidebar, click on [Account Settings](https://exam-insights.com/settings/).

    \{image}
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter and Apply the Access Code">
    In Account Settings, navigate to the My Plan section. Below the plan options, enter your access code in the Upgrade Code box and click Apply to redeem it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirmation">
    After applying the code, you’ll be redirected to the home page, and your account will be upgraded instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Support

Should you run into any issues while redeeming an access code, please don't hesitate to [contact our support team](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/).
