## How to Navigate to Explore

Finding the Explore page is simple, and there are multiple ways to access it.

<Tip>
  Want a better understanding of how Exam Insights is structured? Check out our [Quick Start Guide](/getting_started/quick_start_guide) to see how our pages are organised and where to find key features.
</Tip>

### Option 1: From the Home Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your Home Page">
    This is where all your subjects are listed.

    <Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Subject">
    Click on the subject you want to view Explore for.

    <Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Explore">
    Once inside the subject, you’ll see the Explore option.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

### Option 2: Using the Sidebar

* When you’re inside a subject, the Explore tab is always available in the left sidebar for quick access.

* Simply click on “*Explore*” to open
