### AI Marking Assistant

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

The AI Marking Assistant is like having your own personal examiner, grading your responses instantly against the official marking scheme. No more wondering if your answer was good enough—you’ll get real-time feedback to pinpoint strengths, highlight mistakes, and sharpen your exam technique.

<Info>
  AI Marking Assistant is available on **short** and **extended response** questions (**not multiple choice**)
</Info>

<Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>

To effectively use AI Marking Assistant, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Question">
    Navigate to a past exam question you want to answer. Click on the AI Marking Assistant option to activate the marking feature.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type Your Answer">
    Enter your response in the provided text box, just like you would in an actual exam. Make sure to structure your answer clearly, following standard exam conventions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit for Marking">
    Click Submit to let the AI assess your answer. The AI will compare your response against the official marking scheme.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Instant Feedback">
    The AI will provide a score based on the available marks for the question. You’ll also get feedback on what you did well and what you can improve. If necessary, the AI will highlight missing key points that could earn you extra marks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the AI Marking Assistant**

* **Instant Grading** – No need to wait for a teacher; get immediate feedback on your work.

* **Based on the Official Marking Scheme** – Your answer is assessed using the same criteria examiners use.

* **Detailed Feedback** – Understand where you gained or lost marks and how to improve.

* **Perfect for Self-Study** – Track your progress over time and refine your exam technique.



✅ Aligned to Official Marking Schemes – Get accurate, exam-style grading that mirrors how real markers assess responses.

📊 See Where You Gained & Lost Marks – Break down your results to understand exactly how to improve.

🎯 Fine-Tune Your Exam Technique – Get actionable suggestions to refine your answers before exam day.

🚀 No More Guesswork – Get real-time feedback whenever you need it.

### AI Syllabus Assistant

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Confused by the syllabus? AI Syllabus Assistant helps you cut through the complexity by providing clear, syllabus-aligned explanations. Unlike generic AI tools, it understands the syllabus, ensuring that every response is relevant and targeted.

<Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>

To effectively use AI Syllabus Assistant, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Subject Matter">
    Before asking a question, you first select a unit, topic, subtopic, or learning objective from the syllabus. This helps the AI understand the specific area of the curriculum you are working on.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Your Question">
    Once you’ve selected a Subject Matter, you can ask your question. The AI will generate a response based on past QCAA exam questions, syllabus explanations, and related concepts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Context-Aware Responses">
    The biggest advantage of the AI Syllabus Assistant is that it remembers the Subject Matter you’ve selected. The AI will provide an answer that specifically relates to the syllabus within the QCAA syllabus. This ensures that every answer is relevant and aligned with your studies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the AI Syllabus Assistant**

* **Focused Learning** – No need to filter through irrelevant information.

* **Exam-Aligned Answers** – Responses are directly linked to past QCAA exam questions.

* **Instant Help** – Get explanations in seconds instead of searching through textbooks or notes.

📖 Exam-Focused Learning – No need to sift through unrelated content—every response is directly linked to the syllabus.

🔍 Deep Understanding – Get accurate, well-explained answers without the fluff.

⚡ Faster Study Sessions – Get the clarity you need in seconds, instead of flipping through textbooks.

🎯 Syllabus-Aligned Answers – Feel confident knowing everything you learn is relevant to your exams.

### AI Formula Assistant

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Struggling with complex formulas? The AI Formula Assistant is your go-to for understanding, applying, and manipulating formulas correctly.

No more second-guessing—this AI Assistant references the official QCAA Formula Bheet to give you accurate explanations and step-by-step guidance.

<Frame caption="Caption">
      ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
    </Frame>

To effectively use AI Formula Assistant, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate AI Formula Assistant">
    Open any the formula book, and click on the AI Formula Assistant option to access the feature.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask a Formula-Related Question">
    Ask AI Formula Assistant any formula-related question. For example, you might ask what the variables in a specific formula represent, how to rearrange a formula to solve for a particular variable, or which formula is most suitable for solving a given problem.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive an Accurate Answer">
    The AI will reference the official QCAA formula sheet to provide a clear explanation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the AI Formula Assistant**

* **Accurate Formula Information** – The AI ensures all explanations are based on the official formula sheet.

* **Helps with Understanding** – Get clear explanations of variables, rearrangements, and applications.

* **Quick Formula Lookup** – No need to search through the formula sheet manually; just ask!

* **Supports Problem-Solving** – Learn how to apply formulas correctly in different types of questions.


📚 Formula-Sheet Verified – Every answer is based on official QCAA formulas, so there’s no risk of errors.

🔢 Step-By-Step Breakdowns – Learn how and when to use formulas correctly.

🧠 Deepen Your Understanding – Ask follow-up questions to grasp complex calculations with ease.

⚡ Instant Help, Anytime – No more flipping through pages—just ask and get answers in seconds.
