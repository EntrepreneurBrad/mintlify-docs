## Using Our AI Assistants

### AI Tutor

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Struggling with a tough exam question? AI Tutor is here to help—offering instant, syllabus-aligned explanations tailored to the exact question you’re working on.

It’s not just about getting the answer—it’s about understanding how to get there. Ask a question, get a structured explanation, and follow up until it makes sense.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![7 AI Tutor Overview Jpe](/images/7-AITutorOverview.jpeg)
</Frame>

- **Understands the Marking Scheme** – Knows what QCAA expects and helps you work towards full marks.
- **Syllabus-Aligned** – Keeps you focused on what actually matters for your exams.
- **Instant Help** – No waiting—get clear explanations the moment you need them.
- **Step-by-Step Guidance** – Walks you through the question so you understand every part of the solution.
- **Follow-Up Friendly** – Ask again if something doesn’t make sense—it's a real back-and-forth conversation.

### Grade with AI

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

The Grade with AI is like having your own personal examiner, instantly grading your responses against the official marking scheme.

No more second-guessing—get real-time feedback on what you did well, what needs improvement, and how to secure more marks.

<Info>
  Grade with AI is found on all **short** and **extended response** questions.
</Info>

It’s not just about scoring your answers—it’s about understanding how to improve. Submit your response and get targeted feedback that helps you refine your exam technique.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![35 AI Marking Assistant Jpe](/images/35-AIMarkingAssistant.jpeg)
</Frame>

- **Marks Like a Real Examiner** – Assesses your response using the official QCAA marking scheme.
- **Instant Feedback** – See where you gained and lost marks right away—no waiting.
- **Clear, Actionable Insights** – Highlights exactly what’s missing and how to improve your answer.
- **Fine-Tune Your Exam Technique** – Learn how to structure responses for maximum marks.
- **No More Guesswork** – Get precise, exam-style feedback whenever you need it.

### Syllabus AI

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Syllabus AI breaks down the syllabus into clear, structured explanations—helping you grasp key concepts, learning objectives, and subject matter with ease.

Unlike generic AI tools, this isn’t guesswork—Syllabus AI understands the QCAA syllabus and delivers focused, exam-relevant insights so you can study with confidence.

It’s perfect for:

- Getting an overview of a topic before you start studying.
- Understanding key learning objectives and what’s required.
- Clarifying complex syllabus points with targeted explanations.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
</Frame>

- **Knows Exactly What’s Required** – Breaks down subject matter in line with the syllabus.
- **Covers Key Learning Objectives** – Helps you focus on what you actually need to learn.
- **Clear, Exam-Relevant Explanations** – No fluff—just the content that matters.
- **Supports Deeper Understanding** – Reinforces knowledge so you’re prepared for assessments.
- **Faster, More Effective Studying** – Get instant clarity without digging through textbooks.

### Formula AI

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Struggling with formulas? Formula AI helps you understand, apply, and manipulate formulas with confidence—so you can stop second-guessing and focus on solving problems.

Unlike a search through your textbook, this AI is built for accuracy, referencing the official QCAA Formula Sheet to ensure every explanation is correct and exam-relevant.

It’s perfect for:

- Understanding how formulas work and what each variable represents.
- Learning how to rearrange formulas to solve for different variables.
- Identifying which formula to use for a given problem.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![36 AI Formula Assistant Jpe](/images/36-AIFormulaAssistant.jpeg)
</Frame>

- **Formula-Sheet Focused** – Every answer is based on the official QCAA Formula Book for eavh subject, ensuring 100% accuracy.
- **Breaks Down Every Step** – Learn how and when to apply formulas correctly.
- **Clarifies Complex Calculations** – Ask follow-up questions to fully grasp difficult concepts.
- **No More Guessing** – Get instant explanations instead of flipping through formula books.
