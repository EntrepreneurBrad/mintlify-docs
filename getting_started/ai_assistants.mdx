## How to Use AI Assistants

### AI Tutor

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Our AI Tutor is designed to help you better understand and solve exam questions by providing accurate, syllabus-aligned explanations. Unlike a general AI, the AI Tutor has direct context on the question you’re working on, allowing it to give precise and relevant answers.

To effectively use AI Tutor, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Question">
    Navigate to a past exam question you need help with. Click on the AI Tutor option to activate the assistant for that specific question.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Your Question">
    Ask the AI Tutor a question or request guidance.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive a Targeted Explanation">
    The AI will analyse the question and provide a detailed, structured response based on the syllabus. If you need further clarification, you can ask follow-up questions to dive deeper into specific concepts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the AI Tutor**

* **More Accurate Answers** – Since the AI has the context of the question, its responses are much more relevant than a generic chatbot.

* **Step-by-Step Guidance** – Get detailed explanations on how to solve the problem, rather than just the final answer.

* **Aligned with QCAA Syllabus** – The AI ensures that explanations follow the correct syllabus guidelines.

* **Instant Feedback** – No waiting for a tutor—get help whenever you need it!

***

### AI Marking Assistant

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

The AI Marking Assistant is designed to give you instant feedback on your answers by grading them against the official marking scheme. This allows you to assess your performance, understand where you went wrong, and improve your exam technique.

<Info>
  AI Marking Assistant is on available on **short** and **extended response** questions (**not multiple choice**)
</Info>

To effectively use AI Marking Assistant, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Question">
    Navigate to a past exam question you want to answer. Click on the AI Marking Assistant option to activate the marking feature.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type Your Answer">
    Enter your response in the provided text box, just like you would in an actual exam. Make sure to structure your answer clearly, following standard exam conventions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit for Marking">
    Click Submit to let the AI assess your answer. The AI will compare your response against the official marking scheme.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Instant Feedback">
    The AI will provide a score based on the available marks for the question. You’ll also get feedback on what you did well and what you can improve. If necessary, the AI will highlight missing key points that could earn you extra marks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the AI Marking Assistant**

* **Instant Grading** – No need to wait for a teacher; get immediate feedback on your work.

* **Based on the Official Marking Scheme** – Your answer is assessed using the same criteria examiners use.

* **Detailed Feedback** – Understand where you gained or lost marks and how to improve.

* **Perfect for Self-Study** – Track your progress over time and refine your exam technique.

### Syllabus AI

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Syllabus AI is an intelligent assistant designed to help students understand the QCAA syllabus. The key advantage of the Syllabus AI is its ability to interpret questions within the context of the selected Subject Matter, ensuring accurate and relevant responses.

To effectively use Syllabus AI, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Subject Matter">
    Before asking a question, you first select a unit, topic, subtopic, or learning objective from the syllabus. This helps the AI understand the specific area of the curriculum you are working on.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Your Question">
    Once you’ve selected a Subject Matter, you can type your question. The AI will generate a response based on past QCAA exam questions, syllabus explanations, and related concepts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Context-Aware Responses">
    The biggest advantage of the Syllabus AI is that it remembers the Subject Matter you’ve selected. The AI will provide an answer that specifically relates to the syllabus within the QCAA syllabus. This ensures that every answer is relevant and aligned with your studies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the Syllabus AI**

* **Focused Learning** – No need to filter through irrelevant information.

* **Exam-Aligned Answers** – Responses are directly linked to past QCAA exam questions.

* **Instant Help** – Get explanations in seconds instead of searching through textbooks or notes.

### Formula AI

<Note>
  This feature is free but requires a **free account**, with limited queries per week. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited access.
</Note>

Formula AI is designed to help you understand and apply formulas correctly. It references the official formula sheet to answer any formula-related questions, ensuring accuracy in your calculations and explanations.

To effectively use Formula AI, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Activate Formula AI">
    Open any the formula book, and click on the Formula AI option to access the feature.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask a Formula-Related Question">
    Ask Formula AI any formula-related question. For example, you might ask what the variables in a specific formula represent, how to rearrange a formula to solve for a particular variable, or which formula is most suitable for solving a given problem.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive an Accurate Answer">
    The AI will reference the official QCAA formula sheet to provide a clear explanation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits of Using the Formula AI**

* **Accurate Formula Information** – The AI ensures all explanations are based on the official formula sheet.

* **Helps with Understanding** – Get clear explanations of variables, rearrangements, and applications.

* **Quick Formula Lookup** – No need to search through the formula sheet manually; just ask!

* **Supports Problem-Solving** – Learn how to apply formulas correctly in different types of questions.
