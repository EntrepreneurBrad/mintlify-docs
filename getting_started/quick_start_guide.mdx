Welcome to Exam Insights! Whether you’re here to find past exam questions, track your progress, or get tailored insights, this guide will help you get started.

***

## How to Get Started

Exam Insights is free to use, and you don’t even need an account to get started. Here’s how to make the most of it:

* **Jump in right away** – Many features are available without signing up.

* **Create an account for extra benefits** – If you sign up, we can track your progress and store your selected subjects to personalise your experience, ensuring you always know where you stand.

* **Most features are free** – The core tools, like finding past exam questions, are completely free.

* **Upgrade for extra features** – Advanced features are available in affordable plans ($2–$5/month), but most core features remain free.

<Tip>
Need help signing up? Follow the steps on our [Create Account](/getting_started/create_account) guide.  
</Tip>

## My Subjects

**This is the platform’s home page**

When you sign in, you’ll land on [My Subjects](https://exam-insights.com/home/), your central hub for tracking your subjects and progress.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
</Frame>

Here, you can:
* View all your selected subjects in one place.
* See an overview of your performance, displayed as a percentage for each subject.

Click on any subject to open its dashboard, where you’ll find everything you need for that subject.

***

## Subject Home

Each subject has its own home page, where you can track your progress and access all the tools you need for that subject. Clicking on a subject from the [My Subjects](https://exam-insights.com/home/) page will take you to its home page.

<Frame caption="Caption">
  ![](/images/Untitleddesign.png)
</Frame>

Here, you'll find:
* **Progress Overview** – A graph displaying your question history over time, along with your average rating and it's distribution.
* **Quick Access to All Subject-Specific Features** – This is the central hub for everything related to the subject, including:
  * Explore
  * Questions
  * Insights
  * Performance
  * Glossary
  * Formula and Data Book (for applicable subjects)

### Questions

[Questions](https://exam-insights.com/physics/questions/) is at the heart of Exam Insights, offering a flexible playground for exploring past QCAA external exam questions. It’s more than just a question bank—you have full control over what you see by combining powerful filters to narrow down exactly what you need.

Filter by unit, topic, subtopic, difficulty, cognitive verbs, and more to pinpoint the most relevant questions for your study or teaching focus. Whether you’re searching for a specific type of question or a broader range, everything you need is in one place—including the formula book, marking scheme, and any relevant stimulus.

### Insights

[Insights](https://exam-insights.com/physics/insights/) provides a data-driven playground for analysing past QCAA external exams, helping both students and educators identify trends and focus effectively.

Here, you can explore all available data and apply filters to see exactly what matters to you—whether it’s question frequency, mark distribution, or cognitive verb trends. Every data point is interactive, allowing you to drill down into specific topics, question formats, and difficulty levels, eliminating guesswork so students can study smarter and educators can guide learning more strategically.
