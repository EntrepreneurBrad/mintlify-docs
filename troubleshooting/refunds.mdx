At Exam Insights, we are committed to a transparent and fair refund process. If you believe you meet the eligibility criteria for a refund, please refer to our [Refund Policy](https://help.exam-insights.com/policies/refund-policy) for full details on when refunds are available.

***

## When are Refunds Available?

### Eligibility for Refunds:

* **Major Service Disruptions** – If a significant issue prevents access or functionality for more than 28 days after being reported.

* **Incorrect Purchase** – If you accidentally purchased the wrong plan and notify us within a reasonable timeframe before use.

* **Exceptional Circumstances** – In rare cases, such as duplicate purchases, refunds may be considered at our discretion.

### Refunds Are Not Available For:

* **Change of Mind** – We do not offer refunds if you decide the platform is not for you.

* **Terms of Use Violations** – If an account is terminated due to a policy violation, no refunds will be issued.

* **Partially Used Plans** – Refunds are not provided for plans that have been used or partially accessed.

## How to Request a Refund

If your situation meets the criteria for a refund, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send an Email from Your Registered Email Address">
    All refund requests must be sent from the email associated with your Exam Insights account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Include Your Receipt Number">
    Please reference your receipt number from your purchase confirmation email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Details">
    Briefly explain your reason for requesting a refund, including any relevant screenshots or error messages if applicable.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4">
    Send Your Request to [refunds@exam-insights.com](mailto:refunds@exam-insights.com).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Process">
    Our team will assess your request and respond within a reasonable timeframe. 
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

<Info>
  If approved, your refund will be processed via the original payment method.
</Info>
