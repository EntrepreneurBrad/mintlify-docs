## Our Refund Policy

At Exam Insights, we are committed to providing a transparent and fair refund policy. This page outlines the conditions under which refunds and replacements may be granted for purchases made on exam-insights.com.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact [legal@exam-insights.com](mailto:legal@exam-insights.com).

## When Are Refunds Available?

### Major Software Issues

If you experience a major technical issue that significantly impairs functionality and it remains unresolved for more than 28 days after notifying the Exam Insights team, you may be eligible for:

* A full refund

* A replacement or alternative access

### Minor Software Issues

For smaller technical issues, we will work to fix the problem through updates or patches. If the issue is not resolved within 28 days of being reported, you can request a refund.

### Change of Mind

We do not offer refunds or replacements for change-of-mind purchases. Please ensure you review the product details before completing a purchase.

### Violation of Terms of Use

If your account is terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Use, you will not be eligible for a refund.

## How to Request a Refund

If your situation meets the criteria for a refund, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Us">
    Contact us at [legal@exam-insights.com](mailto:legal@exam-insights.com).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Details">
    Provide details about your issue, including any relevant screenshots or error messages.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review">
    Our team will review your request and respond within a reasonable timeframe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If eligible, your refund will be processed through the original payment method.
