If Exam Insights is running slowly, taking too long to load, or not responding as expected, try the steps below to resolve common performance issues.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for Server Outages">
    * Visit our [Status Page](https://exam-insights.instatus.com/) to check for ongoing issues.

    * If there’s an outage, we are likely working on a fix.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Internet Connection">
    A slow or unstable internet connection can affect performance.

    * Try switching to a different Wi-Fi network or using a wired connection.

    * Run a speed test to check your internet speed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refresh the Page & Restart Your Browser">
    * Close and reopen your browser.

    * Try a hard refresh: Press **Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows)** or **Cmd + Shift + R (Mac)**.

    * Restart your device if the issue persists.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clear Cache & Cookies">
    Old cached data can slow down performance.

    * In **Chrome**: Go to **Settings > Privacy & Security > Clear Browsing Data**.

    * Select **Cached images and files**, then **Clear data**.

    * Restart your browser and try again.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Your Browser">
    Outdated browsers can cause performance issues.

    * Ensure you’re using the latest version of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari.

    * If possible, try accessing Exam Insights from a different browser.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Disable Browser Extensions">
    Some extensions may interfere with Exam Insights.

    * Temporarily disable ad blockers or security extensions.

    * Try using Incognito/Private Mode to see if the issue persists.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

### Still Experiencing Issues?

If you’ve tried these steps and are still facing performance problems, [contact our support team](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/). Please include:

* A brief description of the issue.

* Your browser and device type.

* Screenshots or error messages (if available).

Our team will be happy to assist you!
