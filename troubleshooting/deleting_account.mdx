If you wish to delete your Exam Insights account, please follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Our Support Team">
    Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. To request deletion, email our support team at:&#x20;

    [support@exam-insights.com](mailto:support@exam-insights.com)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Include Your Account Details">
    In your request, please provide:

    * Your **registered email address**

    * Confirmation that you understand deletion is **permanent**
  </Step>

  <Step title="What Happens Next?">
    * All personal data will be **removed** from our system

    * You will **lose access** to any saved progress and resources

    * We will confirm once your account has been successfully deleted
  </Step>
</Steps>
