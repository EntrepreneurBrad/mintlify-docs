### Changing Account Details

To change your account details, start by navigating to the [**Settings**](https://exam-insights.com/settings/) page.&#x20;

From there you can edit details such as your name, school and role.&#x20;

<Note>
  Updating your details will take you through the account setup process again.
</Note>

### Changing Email Address

<Tip>
  Email Addresses cannot be changed through the settings page.
</Tip>

If you need to change your email address, please [Contact Us](https://exam-insights.com/contact-us/).
