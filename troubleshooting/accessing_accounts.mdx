### Resetting Your Password

If you’ve forgotten your password and need to reset it, visit our [Reset Password page](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/password/reset/).&#x20;

Enter your email on the reset page, and we’ll send you an email with a link to reset your password.

If you don’t receive the reset email, check your spam folder or try again after a few minutes. If the issue persists, please [contact our support team](https://exam-insights.com/contact-us/) for assistance.

### Logging into Your Account

To log into an already existing account, go to our [Login page](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/login/).

Here you can enter your email and password to access your account.

### Creating an Account

To create an account, visit our [Sign Up page](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/signup/).
