If you’re having trouble accessing your account, follow the steps below to reset your password, sign in, or create a new account.

***

## Resetting Your Password

<Warning>
  If you sign in with Google, you won’t be able to reset your password through our platform. Instead, reset it through your Google account settings.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit our Reset Password Page">
    If you've forgotten your password, visit the [Reset Password](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/password/reset/) page to start the process.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your Details">
    Enter your registered email address. We'll send you a password reset link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Didn’t Receive the Email?">
    If you don’t receive the reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder. If you still don’t see it, try again or [contact our support team](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/) for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

## Logging into Your Account

To sign in to an already existing account, go to our [Sign In](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/login/) page.

If you receive an error or are unable to sign in, ensure you’re using the correct email associated with your account. If you’re still having trouble, try resetting your password.

***

## Creating an Account

If you’re new to Exam Insights, you can sign up by following the instructions on our [Create Account](/getting_started/create_account) guide.
