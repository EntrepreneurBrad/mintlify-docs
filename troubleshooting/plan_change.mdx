## **Upgrading or Changing Your Plan**  

Exam Insights offers **one-time annual payments**—plans do **not auto-renew** or follow a subscription model.  

---

### **🔹 Upgrading Your Plan**  
✅ You can **upgrade anytime** through the **[Pricing Page](/pricing)** or your **[Account Settings](/account/settings)**.  
✅ The **difference in price** between your current plan and the new plan will apply.  
✅ Your upgraded plan will **immediately unlock new features**.  

---

### **🔹 Downgrading Your Plan**  
❌ **Downgrades are not available** once a plan has been purchased.  
❌ If you need a different plan next year, you can **choose a new plan at renewal**.  

---

### **🔹 Need Help?**  
If you're unsure which plan is right for you, check out our **[Pricing Page](/pricing)** or **[contact our support team](/contact-support)**.
