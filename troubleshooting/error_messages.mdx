## **Troubleshooting Common Error Messages**  

If you’ve encountered an error message while using Exam Insights, don’t worry! Below are explanations of what these errors mean and what you can do to resolve them.

---

### **🚫 404 – Page Not Found**  
**What it means:** The page you’re looking for doesn’t exist or has been moved.  
**How to fix:**  
✅ Check the URL for typos.  
✅ Return to the **[home page](/)** and try navigating from there.  

---

### **⚠️ 500 – Internal Server Error**  
**What it means:** Something went wrong on our end.  
**How to fix:**  
✅ Wait a moment and try refreshing the page.  
✅ If the issue continues, **[report it to us](/contact-support)**.  

---

### **🛑 400 – Bad Request**  
**What it means:** The request sent to the server was invalid.  
**How to fix:**  
✅ Double-check any forms or inputs you’re submitting.  
✅ Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser.  

---

### **🔒 403 – Forbidden (Access Denied)**  
**What it means:** You don’t have permission to access this page.  
**How to fix:**  
✅ Ensure you are logged into the correct account.  
✅ If you believe this is an error, **[contact support](/contact-support)**.  

---

### **🚀 429 – Too Many Requests**  
**What it means:** You’ve made too many requests in a short period.  
**How to fix:**  
✅ Wait a few minutes before trying again.  
✅ Reduce the number of rapid requests to avoid rate limits.  

---

### **🔁 Session Conflict**  
**What it means:** Your session has expired, or another device has logged into your account.  
**How to fix:**  
✅ Log in again.  
✅ If you didn’t log in from another device, reset your password as a precaution.  

---

If you're still experiencing issues, **[contact our support team](/contact-support)** for further assistance.
