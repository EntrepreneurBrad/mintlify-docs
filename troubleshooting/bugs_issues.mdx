## **Report a Bug or Provide Feedback**  

We value your input! If you've encountered a **technical issue, bug, or have feedback** to improve Exam Insights, please reach out to us.

### **📌 How to Report an Issue**  
1️⃣ **Describe the problem clearly** – Include as much detail as possible.  
2️⃣ **Provide screenshots (if applicable)** – This helps us identify the issue faster.  
3️⃣ **Tell us where you encountered it** – Include the page or feature where the problem occurred.  

🔗 **[Report a Bug or Issue](/contact-support)**

Your insights help shape Exam Insights. If you have **suggestions, feature requests, or general feedback**, let us know.
