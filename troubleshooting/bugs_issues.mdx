If you've encountered a technical issue, bug, or have feedback to improve Exam Insights, please reach out to us.

### How to Report a Bug or Issue

You can report a Bug or Issue on our [Contact Us](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/) Page.

Follow these steps when reporting a bug or issue:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe the problem clearly">
    Include as much detail as possible.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide screenshots (if applicable)">
    This helps us identify the issue faster.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tell us where you encountered it">
    Include the page or feature where the problem occurred.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

### Share Your Feedback

Your insights help shape Exam Insights. If you have suggestions, feature requests or general feedback, let us know [here](https://exam-insights.com/contact-us/).

### Urgent Support

For urgent support, visit our [Contact Us](https://exam-insights.com/contact-us/) page.
