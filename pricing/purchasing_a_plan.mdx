## Understanding Exam Insights Plans

Exam Insights offers a one-time purchase plan that provides full access for an entire school year—there are no recurring subscriptions or automatic renewals. Once purchased, your plan remains active until the end of the academic year.

With an Exam Insights plan, you gain:

* Upgraded access to all subjects

* Enhanced study tools and insights

* A streamlined exam preparation experience

To get started, simply choose the plan that suits your needs and follow the steps below.

***

## How to Purchase a Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    To purchase a plan, an Exam Insights account is required. If you do not have an account, please visit our [**Sign Up**](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/signup/) page to create one. If you already have an account, you can sign in via the [**Sign In**](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/login/) page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Visit the Pricing Page and Choose a Plan">
    Visit our [Pricing](https://exam-insights.com/pricing/) page to explore available plans and select the one that best fits your needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Payment Details & Confirm Your Purchase">
    Review your order details before securely completing your purchase at checkout.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Account Update">
    Once your payment is processed, your account will be updated automatically with your new plan’s features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive an Email Confirmation">
    You will receive a confirmation email with your purchase details and receipt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This is a one-time purchase for a full year, with no subscription or automatic renewal.
</Note>
