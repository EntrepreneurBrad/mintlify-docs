## How to Purchase a Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    To purchase a plan, you’ll need an account.&#x20;

    If you don’t have an account yet, visit our [**Sign Up**](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/signup/) page to create one, or log in via the [**Login**](https://exam-insights.com/accounts/login/) page if you already have an account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Visit the Pricing Page and Choose a Plan">
    Go to our [Pricing](https://exam-insights.com/pricing/) page to explore available plans and select the one that best fits your needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Payment Details">
    Provide your payment information securely at checkout.

    <Note>
      Something to do with what payment we take idk
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Your Purchase">
    Review your order, then confirm your purchase.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Account Update">
    Once your payment is processed, your account will be updated automatically with your new plan’s features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive an Email Confirmation">
    You’ll receive a confirmation email with details about your purchase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This is a one-time purchase for a full year, with no subscription or automatic renewal.
</Note>
