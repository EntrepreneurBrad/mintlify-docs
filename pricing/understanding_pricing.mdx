At Exam Insights, we believe in providing flexible and transparent pricing that works for students, educators, and schools. Our plans are designed to give you access to our powerful features without hidden fees or long-term commitments.

***

## Our Plans

We offer three pricing options to fit different needs:

• **Free Plan -** Get access to 2,200+ QCAA past exam questions, video solutions, and essential resources at no cost.

• **Plus Plan** - Unlock advanced question filtering, insights into past exam trends, and keyword search tools.

• **Pro Plan** - Get full access to all features, including our AI Assistants for tutoring and feedback, plus advanced performance tracking to pinpoint your strengths and areas for improvement.

The core differences between each plan can be found on our [Pricing](https://exam-insights.com/pricing/) page.

<Check>
  No credit card information is required for our Free Plan
</Check>

***

## No Subscriptions, No Auto-Renewals

We operate on a one-time annual payment model, meaning you won’t be automatically charged when your plan expires. At the end of your plan, you can decide whether to renew and select the plan that suits you best.
