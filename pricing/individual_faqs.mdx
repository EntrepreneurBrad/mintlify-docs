### Can I upgrade from the Plus plan to Pro later without paying the full $59?

Yes! You will only pay the difference between the two plans rather than the full amount.

### Do I need a credit card to try Exam Insights?

No, you don’t need a credit card for our free plan. You can start using it without any payment information.

### Is Exam Insights a subscription service?

No, Exam Insights is a one-time annual payment for a full school year. Renewal is optional each year.

### Is there a free trial?

We don’t offer a traditional free trial, but our free plan lets you explore core features at no cost, with no time limit.

### Can I upgrade from the free plan to a paid plan?

Yes! You can upgrade anytime to unlock premium features, and the upgrade applies for the remainder of the school year.

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, refunds are available in specific cases, such as technical issues preventing platform access.

### Why are payments structured around the school year?

We charge on a school-year basis to ensure uninterrupted access to resources during the academic year. This system provides flexibility, allowing you to choose if and when to renew.

### I just purchased a plan, but my account isn’t upgraded yet. What should I do?

Upgrades typically process within a few minutes. If you don’t see the change, try refreshing the page or logging out and back in. If the issue persists, contact support with your receipt ID.

### Can I transfer my subscription to another account?

No, subscriptions are non-transferable. Each account must have its own subscription.
