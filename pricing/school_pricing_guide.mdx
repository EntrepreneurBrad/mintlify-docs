## How to Purchase a School License

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a Quote">
    You can inquire about pricing in two ways:

    * Check our Pricing Page to view school licensing rates.

    * Request a quote by [contacting us](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/).

    <Tip>
      The pricing on the pricing page and quote will be the same
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request an Invoice">
    Once you’re ready to proceed, request an invoice by reaching out to us via our [**Contact Page**](https://exam-insights.com/help-and-resources/). We’ll generate an invoice and send it to you for payment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Make a Payment">
    We accept multiple payment methods for convenience:

    • **Direct Debit**

    • **Credit/Debit Card**

    • **Link**

    Once the payment is received, we will process your order.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Access Codes">
    After your payment is confirmed, we will provide access codes that can be reedemed on individual accounts. These codes grant users access to the licensed plan.

    To learn how to apply access codes, visit our [**Reedeming Access Codes**](https://help.exam-insights.com/getting_started/access_codes) page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This is a one-time purchase for a full year, with no subscription or automatic renewal.
</Note>
