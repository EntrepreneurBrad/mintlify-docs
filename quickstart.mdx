### About Exam Insights

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Exam Insights?" icon="book">
    Exam Insights equips Queensland's Year 12 students and educators with powerful external exam question banks and data-driven insights to maximise results and save time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What subjects and how many exam questions can I access on Exam Insights?" icon="books">
    You can access over 2,200 exam questions across 22 QCAA subjects on Exam Insights. This includes: ccounting, Ancient History, Biology, Business, Chemistry, Digital Solutions, Earth & Environmental Science, Economics, Engineering, General Mathematics, Geography, German, Legal Studies, Marine Science, Mathematical Methods, Modern History, Physical Education, Physics, Psychology, Specialist Mathematics, Study of Religion
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Exam Insights aligned with the QCAA syllabus?" icon="book">
    Yes, Exam Insights is aligned with the latest 2019 QCAA syllabus and will be updated to the 2025 syllabus when appropriate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often is the question database updated?" icon="update">
    The question database is updated at minimum when the previous external exams are released by the QCAA, which typically occurs mid-year.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I access Exam Insights outside of Queensland?" icon="globe">
    Currently, Exam Insights is available to students and educators in Queensland. Expansion to additional regions is planned for 2025.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Using Exam Insights

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I access Unit 1 and 2 questions?" icon="books">
    At the moment, our filters display questions from Units 3 and 4, as these are the primary focus of our current database. Unit 1 and 2 questions are not yet available across all subjects. However, we are actively working on adding Unit 1 and 2 content for specific subjects like Biology, Physics, and Specialist Maths by onboarding alternative sequence papers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I print the exam questions I have chosen?" icon="print">
    Not currently, but this feature is coming as part of Create. We understand the importance of practicing on paper, and we’re working on adding this functionality soon.
