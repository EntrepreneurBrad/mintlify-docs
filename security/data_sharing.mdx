We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. However, some data may be shared in limited cases.

These limited cases include:

* **Anonymised Performance Data** – Used for educational research and platform improvements.

* **School-Level Insights** – Aggregated data may be shared with schools but never linked to individuals.

* **Legal Requirements** – If required by law, we may disclose certain data.

Your data remains confidential unless explicit consent is provided.
