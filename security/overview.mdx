At Exam Insights, your privacy is not just a priority — it’s a foundational part of how we build our platform. We’re committed to protecting personal and academic information through robust security, responsible data practices, and complete transparency.

We do **not** sell or share your personal information with third parties. We only collect data that’s necessary to enhance your exam preparation experience.

---

## Our Privacy Principles

* **Transparency** – We clearly outline how we collect, store, and use data.
* **Data Protection** – Strong encryption and security measures keep your information safe.
* **User Control** – You can request to delete your account at any time.

---

## Built-in Protection Measures

- **End-to-End Encryption** – Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
- **Controlled Access** – Only authorised personnel can access personal data.
- **Privacy by Design** – Our platform is built with your privacy in mind.
