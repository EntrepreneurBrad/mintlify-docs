Exam Insights is committed to protecting user data through secure encryption, access controls, and industry best practices. Our platform follows strict security protocols to ensure that all personal and academic information remains protected.
At Exam Insights, we do not sell or share personal information with third parties. We only collect the data necessary to enhance user experience and provide better exam preparation tools.

What We Do to Protect Your Data:

✅ Encryption & Secure Storage – All user data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
✅ Access Controls – We restrict access to sensitive information to authorized personnel only.
✅ Regular Security Audits – Our systems are continuously monitored for vulnerabilities.
✅ Compliance – We follow the Australian Privacy Act and adhere to data security best practices.

Key Privacy Principles:

✅ Transparency – We clearly outline how we collect, store, and use data.
✅ Data Protection – Strong encryption and security measures keep your information safe.
✅ User Control – You can request to delete your account at any time.

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy. exam-insights.com/policies/privacy_policy/
