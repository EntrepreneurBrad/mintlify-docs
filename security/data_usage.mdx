We use collected data only to enhance learning experiences and improve Exam Insights.

We use data to:

* **Personalised Study Insights** – Helping students track progress, strengths, and weaknesses.

* **Improved Platform Features** – Identifying trends to refine Exam Insights tools.

* **Security & Compliance** – Ensuring all activity remains protected under privacy regulations.

We never use data for advertising or third-party sales.
