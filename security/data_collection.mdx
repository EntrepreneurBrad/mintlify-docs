Exam Insights collects certain information to personalise study experiences and improve our platform.&#x20;

The types of data we collect include:

* **Personal Information:** First name, school, email, subjects selected, and user role.

* **Usage Data:** Performance tracking, question ratings, and time spent studying.

* **Technical Information:** Browser type, device, and page interactions for platform optimisation.

We never sell or share this data without consent.
