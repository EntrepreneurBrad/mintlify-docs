## **Types of Data We Collect and Why**

Exam Insights collects certain information to personalize study experiences and improve our platform.

* **Personal Information:** First name, school, email, subjects selected, and user role.

* **Usage Data:** Performance tracking, question ratings, and time spent studying.

* **Technical Information:** Browser type, device, and page interactions for platform optimization.

We never sell or share this data without consent.
